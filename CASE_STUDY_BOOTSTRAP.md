- Produce the case study document
- Include: medical data, output data, structural analysis,
  the enabling hypothesis evaluation, and implications
- Format: formal research document (not clinical note)
- Tone: rigorous, respectful, honest, and willing to hold
  contradiction without resolving it prematurely

- Present to subject for review
- Subject corrects, amends, or expands
- Iterate until the subject confirms accuracy
